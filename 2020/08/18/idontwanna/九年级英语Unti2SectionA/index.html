Period 1

in,after,later的辨析

in+时间段，表示“（以现在为起点）一段时间以后”，常与一般将来时态连用。
after+时间点，表示具体某个时间以后，可与多种时态连用。
after+时间段，表示（以过去某个时间为起点）一段时间以后，常与一般过去时态连用
时间段+later，表示多少时间以后，可与多种时态连用。

现在完成时

Chinese people have been celebrating the Mid Autumn Festival and enjoying mooncakes for centuries.

本句使用了现在完成进行时态，表示过去动作发生一直持续到现在仍在继续，结构为 have been doing sth

例句：

I have been working here for 2 years.

我在这里工作了2年。

现在完成时和现在完成进行时的区别

现在完成时强调完成和影响，现在完成进行时强调未完成和动作。

例句：

He has painted the room.

他已经粉刷过房间了。(影响：墙壁是干净的)

He has been painting the room.

他一直在粉刷房间。(还未粉刷完)

现在完成时陈述事实，现在完成进行时表感情色彩。

例句：

What have you done?

你做什么了？(仅是问问)

Where have you been? I have been waiting for you in the rain for two hours.

你去哪了？我在雨中等了你两个小时。(不满)

Period 3

Language points

Chinese people have been celebrating the Mid Autumn Festival and enjoying mooncakes for centuries.

本句使用了现在完成进行时态，表示过去动作发生一直持续到现在仍在继续，结构为 have been doing sth

例句：

I have been working here for 2 years.

我在这里工作了2年。

Mooncakes are in the shape of a full moon on the Mid-Autumn.

English 中文
in the shape of… 呈现……形状
keep in shape 保持体形
Look.The cake is in the shape of a heart. 看，这个蛋糕呈心形。
I like shaping sand into different objects. 我喜欢把沙子堆成不同物体的形状。

shape还可以作动词，意为“使成为……形状”

They carry people’s wishes to the families they love and miss.

English 中文
carry sth to sb 把……带给……

the families they love and miss是定语从句，省略that，替代families的that做从句中love and miss的宾语。

… is the most touching

English 中文
touching(=moving) adj.动人的；感人的

例句：

This is the most touching story I have ever heard.

这是我听到过的最动人的故事。

His farewell speech was very touching.

他的告别演讲非常感人。

知识链接：

English 中文
touch v. 触摸；感动；触及；接触

例句：

Don’t touch the paint until it’s dry.

油漆未干, 切勿触摸。

His sad story touched our hearts.

他的悲惨的故事深深打动了我们的心。

The peak seems to touch the sky.

山峰似乎已触及天空。

After Hou Yi shot down the nine suns, a goddess gave him magic medicine to thank him.

shoot down击落，shoot的过去式为shot

medicine不可数名词

to thank him做目的状语

thank(v.) 后直接跟宾语，表示感谢某人。常用语句型：

Thank you for sth/doing sth
thanks(n./int.)
Thanks for sth./doing sth

whoever[huːˈevə(r)] pron.无论谁；不管什么人

Whoever took this could live forever, and Hou Yi planned to take it …

(1)这句话里的主语是Whoever took this…, 是一个主语从句，相当于Anyone who took this…

eg：

Whoever wants the book may have it.

谁想要这本书都可以拿。

Whoever breaks this law deserves a fine.

违反本法者应予以罚款。

(2)用于引导让步状语从句，相当于no matter who，其意为“不论什么人”。

Whoever you ask，the answer is always the same.

不管你问谁，答案都一样。

Pang Meng, tried to steal[stiːl] (v.偷，窃取) the medicine …

过去式stole,过去分词stolen

steal…from…从……偷……

e.g.

I don’t know who stole my money.

我不知道谁偷了我的钱。

He stole some flowers from the garden.

他从花园里偷走了一-些花。

知识链接

辨析：steal / rob

从意思上讲steal表示偷窃的意思，而rob表示抢劫的意思；
从搭配上来讲，steal sth. from sb. / sth. ;
而rob则用rob sb. / sth. of sth.

例如：

He stole money from the rich to give it to the poor.
They robbed the bank of one million dollars.

Chang’e refused to give it to him … … became light and flew up to the sky.

refuse to do sth 拒绝做某事

eg:

She refused to accept that there was a problem.

她拒不承认有问题存在。

I politely refused their invitation.

我礼貌地回绝了他们的邀请。

refuse sb sth 拒绝给（所需之物）

She would never refuse her kids anything.

她对孩子们百依百顺。

Hou Yi was so sad that he called out her name to the moon every night.

English 中文
call out sth to sth 朝……大声呼喊……
so+adj+that+从句 如此……以至于……

He calls out his son’s name to that boy.

他朝那个男孩大声呼叫他儿子的姓名。

lay[leɪ] v.放置，安置，产蛋

过去式：laid 过去分词：laid

lay out摆开；布置

e.g.

The hen laid an egg yesterday.

昨天这只鸡下了一个蛋。

Lay out the map on the table. Let’s have a look.

把地图在桌子上展开，让我们看一看

eg：

Don’t lay your coat on the bed.

不要把你的外衣放在床上。

The small town lies among the mountains.

小镇位于群山之中。

I’m sorry I lied to you.=I’m sorry I told a lie to you.

我很抱歉向你撒了谎。

Don’t lie in the bed all the morning.

别一个上午都躺在床上。

tradition[trəˈdɪʃn] n.传统 & traditional adj.传统的

The tradition of birthday parties started in Europe a long time ago.

生日聚会的传统开始于很久以前的欧洲。

More and more people have realized the importance of spreading our traditional culture.

越来越多的人已经意识到传播传统文化的重要性。

… admire the moon and share mooncakes with their families

English 中文
admire[ədˈmaɪə(r)] v.欣赏；钦佩；仰慕
admire sb./sth. 欣赏某人/某事
admire sb. for (doing) sth. 因(做)某事而钦佩某人

e.g.

He stood back to admire the work of art.

他退后几步欣赏这件艺术品。

I admire the doctor for saving the girl’s life.

我因为医生救了女孩的命而仰慕他。
